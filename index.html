Crystal-Oss Bone Graft

origin from bovine bone mineral and manufactured via a distinctive process, crystal-oss TM bone grafting particulate has the crystalline structure of neat natural hydroxyapatite, resemble the structure of intact human bone (calcium deficient ). crystal-oss TM allows for a blend of porous structure for vascular access with a roughened surface that is advantage to cell adhesion and subsequent bone matrix deposition

An Osteoconductive Matrix

surgeons have biocompatible and safe osteoconductive matrix used new bone formation in defects

Crystal-oss Indications:

  1. Socket Preservation
  2. Ridge Augmentation
  3. Dental Implantology
  4. Periosurgery
  5. Reconstruction of bone defects following excision of benign tumors and crystals.
  6. Sinus lifting
  7. Defects following removal of autogenous bone in orthopedic surgery and craniofacial surgery.

Crystal-oss Benefits:

  1. Osteoconductive matrix is favorable to bone formation
  2. Porosity of particle allows for revascularization
  3. Particle structure keep to maintain space
  4. Easy handling of material allows for perfect graft placement
